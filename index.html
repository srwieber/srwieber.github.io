About Me

I'm a Front End Web Developer that loves to enhance the user experience.

I'm passionate about creating sites that are easy to navigate, accessible, professional, responsive and error free.

I have been modifying sites since 2010 when I discovered my passion for enhancing my online fantasy football league on myfantasyleague.com. In 2012, I began modifying the content on our league forum on forumotion for our members to enjoy. To this day, I still enjoy creating new features and simplifying the process of navigating the websites.

I love creating sites from scratch and seeing what this beautiful technology can do for us while we surf the web. I also enjoy using front end JavaScript frameworks and Libraries to improve the functionality of larger projects.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring projects created by other web developers. When I'm not online, I enjoy spending time with my family. We enjoy the benefits of the local Pacific Northwest scenery.
